# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Tremor project!

There are many ways to contribute as a developer, or as a technical writer
and we have documented the primary methods on our [contributors guide](https://www.tremor.rs/community/governance/Contributing).

Please note that all work on Tremor is subject to a [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) which is
a requirement for all members of the Tremor Community to uphold.

- The Tremor Team
